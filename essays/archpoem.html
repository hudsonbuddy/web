I never knew this,
I fought the dark, the light, the warm, the chilling.
I would never be willing to take this ride.
This last train back home.

I believed that the train would come and pass,
never to come and pass again.
Again and again it has passed,
But I'll never come again.

I'll be strong, I'll be unbreakable, but I'll never be one.
I'll believe, I'll hope, but I'll never be with one.

Written September 27th, 2005
